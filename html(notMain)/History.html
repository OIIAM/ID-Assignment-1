Sixteen Era

On February 11,2015, J.Y. Park, founder of JYPE, announced that the lineup of the upcoming seven memebr girl group will be decided through Sixteen, a competition survival show that would air on Mnet later that year
The show began on May 5th and ended on July 7th with Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, and Chaeyoung being selected as the seven Twice members.
Park then announced that the group size will eb increased to nine instead of seven by adding Tzuyu, known as the crowd favourite, and Momo, who was added by park himself as he felt that the group needed someone with her performance abilities.

The Story Begins!

Twice made their official debut on October 20th, 2015, with the release of their EP The story begins, simultaneously with its lead single, "Like Ooh-Ahh" alongside a live show to promote it.
The group initially revieved lukewarm reception, "Like Ooh-Ahh" would reach its peak position of number 10 on the Gaon Digital Chart, three months after the song's original release.
The music video for the group's single hit 50 million views on YouTube within five months of their debut.
The group's early success earned them a "Best New Female Artist" award at the 2015 Mnet Asian Music Awards held on December 2nd.
